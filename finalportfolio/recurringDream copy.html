recurring dream

as my eyelids become heavy and i lose alertness, my body lays still, but my mind arrives in another dimension. sitting in an unknown car, my eyes turn towards the window light as the landscape rushes past the pane. i look towards the driver, suprised to see a small child on a booster seat driving the car. now worried, i sit in silence as the unlicensed driver swerves past lane lines. before i get to experience my fate, my eyelids jerk open...
