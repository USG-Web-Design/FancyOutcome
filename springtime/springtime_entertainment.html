If the weather isn't looking so hot, don't worry! Luckily, there are also plenty of indoor options for entertainment and fun in Washington. Keep scrolling to learn more.

#1 Concerts

Concerts are arguably one of the best things to do on a cold, rainy night, and luckily, Washington has a lot of those! From The Weeknd to the Backseat Lovers, you can buy tickets for any genre of music at lots of different price ranges. Some well-known venues include the Showbox, the Tacoma Dome and the Paramount Theatre.


#2 Seattle Art Museum

The Seattle Art Museum has some of the best art collections around! They are currently holding and Asian Art Exhibit, but almost always have a huge range of artwork from all over the world. SAM is conveniently located right in downtown Seattle, so you can walk right to multiple other fun spots like Pike Place and the Selfie Museum.


#3 Theo Chocolate Tour

Located in Fremont (just north of Seattle), Theo Chocolate factory has been around since 2005 and was the first ethically sourced chocolate factory in the area. Book an hour-long tour of their Seattle-based factory and learn all about chcolate-making (and eating) for just $14. Best of all, the tour includes lots of samples and snacks ;)
